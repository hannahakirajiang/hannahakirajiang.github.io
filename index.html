About me

With 5 years'+ experience in design + dev,
I create efficient, yet delightful interfaces to make technology friendly.

My name is Hannah Jiang. I am a UIUX designer with expertise in interaction design, info viz, and design leadership.

While studying IT and working with front-end back in undergrad, I discovered UX design when I was looking for a way to combine creative problem solving with my technical background.

My enthusiasm for design stems from a simple train of thought, I want to create timeless experiences for people. Helping people accomplish their goals or making them smile is a fulfilling feeling to me.

In my free time, I enjoy Photography, Good Movies, hiking, Boxing & Watching UFC, Watching anime, Playing guitar, Learning about Social Science & Human Behavior, Sunny days, Reading, Art, Humor, and Hanging out.
